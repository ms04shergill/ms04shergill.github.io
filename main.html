About The School

Enver Creek Secondary School is a public High school in Surrey. Enver Creek is a part of School district 36. The principal of the school is Mr.Kilpatrick and the two vice are Mr.Magnusson and Mrs.Sadra.The main classes are English,PE,Socials Studies,Math,French, and Science.There are more electives but the main one's are Woodwork,Drama,Computers and Photography.Enver Creek is a great school to go too. All Enver Creek stuents have an equal chance to suceed and have every opportunity to make themselves not only a better student but an all around person in general.
